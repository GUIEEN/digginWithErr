#### __# Finding a word__
* `/` or `?` + a word for search + enter !
* __n__ : search backwards, next occurrence of the word
* __N__ : search forwards
* Searching for the current word
  - `*` - search forwards
  - `#` - search backwards





#### __# Undo and Redo__
* `u` / `ctrl + R`
